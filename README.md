# Hospital-ER-Analysis-POWER-BI
# 🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

Emergency Rooms (ER) are one of the most critical departments in any hospital. Managing patient flow, reducing wait times, and ensuring high patient satisfaction are essential for delivering quality healthcare.

This project analyzes **Emergency Room patient data** to uncover operational insights such as:

- Patient wait times
- Admission trends
- Demographic distribution
- Department referrals
- Patient satisfaction

Using **Power BI**, the raw hospital data was transformed into an **interactive analytics dashboard** that helps hospital administrators monitor ER performance and identify improvement opportunities.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Emergency departments often face:

- Long patient wait times
- Overcrowding
- Inefficient patient routing
- Decreasing patient satisfaction

Hospital management needs a **data-driven system** to monitor ER operations and quickly identify performance bottlenecks.

This project aims to answer questions such as:

- How long are patients waiting in the ER?
- Which departments receive the most referrals?
- What demographic groups visit the ER most frequently?
- How satisfied are patients with ER services?
- What patterns exist in patient admissions?

---

# 🗂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains **Emergency Room patient records** with the following attributes:

| Column | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Patient ID | Unique identifier for each patient |
| Patient Admission Date | Date and time of ER admission |
| Patient Gender | Gender of the patient |
| Patient Age | Age of the patient |
| Patient Race | Ethnicity / race of the patient |
| Department Referral | Department referred after ER visit |
| Patient Admission Flag | Indicates if patient was admitted |
| Patient Satisfaction Score | Patient satisfaction rating |
| Patient Wait Time | Time patient waited before treatment |
| Patients CM | Case management indicator |

# 📈 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ ER Performance Metrics

Key performance indicators such as:

- Total Patients
- Average Wait Time
- Admission Rate
- Average Satisfaction Score

These metrics provide a quick snapshot of ER efficiency.

---

## 2️⃣ Patient Demographics Analysis

Visualizations showing:

- Age distribution
- Gender distribution
- Race distribution

This helps hospitals understand the **patient population they serve**.

---

## 3️⃣ Department Referral Analysis

Displays which departments receive the highest referrals from the ER such as:

- Cardiology
- Orthopedics
- Neurology
- General Medicine

This insight helps hospitals allocate **resources and staff effectively**.

---

## 4️⃣ Patient Wait Time Analysis

Analyzes:

- Average wait times
- Wait time trends
- Peak ER hours

This helps hospital administrators identify **operational inefficiencies**.

---

## 5️⃣ Patient Satisfaction Insights

Tracks satisfaction scores to determine:

- Service quality
- Patient experience
- Areas requiring improvement
